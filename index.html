*LESSON 4 SENTENCES
Sentences: Present, Past, Future, and Using FINISH:
1. John already telephoned Jack.
2. I will buy paper later.
3. Last night grandmother died.
4. In the future, I will visit Washington D.C.
5. I wrote the letter.
6. Yesterday, it was cold.
7. Tomorrow my sister will buy a cookie.
8. My sister recently found a dog.
9. She went to a hearing school before.
10. My brother has read the book.
11. Mother will stay one week.
12. I'll make a large cookie later on.
13. I remembered your name yesterday. Now I forgot what your name is.
14. He will learn sign language.
15. I cooked all day.

Part 2:
1. A long time ago, he liked to work. Now he doesn't like to work.
2. I used to practice signing. Now I don't.
3. I remember your name yesterday. Now I don't remember it.
4. I'm finished working.
5. Father made cookies.
6. She went to hearing school before.
7. My brother has read the book.
8. My sign language has already improved.
9. She visited Washington a long time ago.
10. I'll see the movie tomorrow.

Family Pnemonics 





    

        👨 View Masculine Family Signs (Forehead Area)
        

            
                

                    Sign
                    Action
                    Mnemonic / Trick
                

            
            
                
ManForehead → Chest"The Beard": Tracing a long beard from face to chest.

                
FatherTap forehead (open 5)"The Head": Tapping the "head" of the family.

                
HusbandForehead → Clasp hands"Hand-Holding Man": The boy you chose to hold hands with.

                
BoyGrab air at forehead"The Cap Grab": Tipping the bill of a baseball cap.

                
SonBoy sign → Cradle arms"The Baby Boy": A boy who is also your baby.

                
BrotherBoy sign → Palms meet"The Same Cap": Two guys from the same family (hat).

                
GrandpaFather sign + 2 bounces"Double Generation": Moving further from the head.

                
UncleShake "U" at temple"U" is for Uncle. High up for men.

                
NephewShake "N" at temple"N" is for Nephew. High up for boys.

            
        

    


    

        👩 View Feminine Family Signs (Chin Area)
        

            
                

                    Sign
                    Action
                    Mnemonic / Trick
                

            
            
                
WomanChin → Chest"The Tender Heart": From a mother's kiss down to her heart.

                
MotherTap chin (open 5)"The Kiss": Tapping where a mother kisses a child's chin.

                
WifeChin → Clasp hands"Hand-Holding Girl": The girl you chose to hold hands with.

                
GirlSlide thumb down jaw"The Pretty Face": Highlighting an adorable face or jawline.

                
DaughterGirl sign → Cradle arms"The Baby Girl": A girl who is also your baby.

                
SisterGirl sign → Palms meet"The Same Bonnet": Two girls from the same family.

                
GrandmaMother sign + 2 bounces"Double Generation": Moving further from the chin.

                
AuntShake "A" at chin"A" is for Aunt. Low down for women.

                
NieceShake "N" at chin"N" is for Niece. Low down for girls.
